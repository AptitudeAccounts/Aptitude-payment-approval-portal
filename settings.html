User Management

User accounts are created and managed in the Firebase Authentication console. Each user's role is automatically determined from their email address (see ROLE_MAP in js/firebase/firebase-config.js). The three predefined portal accounts are listed below.

NameEmailRolePermissions
Accounts Teamaccounts@aptitude.aeAccounts Create / edit own requests, view own status, download own PDF
Adminadmin@aptitude.aeAdministrator Full access: approve, reject, hold, reports, settings, user management
Operations Manageroperations@aptitude.aeOperations Manager Approve, reject, hold, reports (no settings/user management)
